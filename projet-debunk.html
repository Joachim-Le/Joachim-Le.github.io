Bullet Hell - Unity - C# - 2025
10 students - 4 days

  • This project was part of a four-day game jam organized by the Women in Games association.
  • I worked in a team of 2 Gameplay Programmers, 4 Game Designers, 3 Game Artists and 1 Sound Designer.
  • The theme was "Challenging Stereotypes."
  • Debunk is a bullet hell game in which you face a colleague whose thinking is a little too stereotyped.
  • Counter their arguments by throwing their bullets back at them in an attempt to win the debate!

My Contributions

  • Programming & Tool Creation
    • Coded several pattern types such as linear, circular, sinusoid, and even a laser!
    • Used Unity's scriptable objects to make it easy for Game Designers to create, customize and integrate their own patterns.
  • Asset & Juiciness Integration
    • Incorporated particle effects to improve the game's visuals.

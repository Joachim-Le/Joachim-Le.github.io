3D Fighting Game Prototype

Unity - C# - 2025
1 student - 2 weeks

  • This project is a 20-hour work made during my 2nd year at ISART.

  • It's a 3D Fighting Game Prototype with a heavy inspiration from Dragon Ball Sparking Zero.

  • I recreated features such as :
    • Moving around the opponent
    • Dashing
    • Teleporting
    • Shooting a Kamehameha, the iconic laser beam from Dragon Ball !

My Contributions

  • 3D Space Movements & Camera

    • Coded player movements to always take in account where the enemy is.

    • Implemented other classic fighting game movements.

    • Managed the camera to give a satisfying feeling during dash.
  • Effective Visual Effects

    • Recreated the teleport effect from Dragon Ball Z.

    • Designed shaders and particle effects for Kamehameha.

    • Added speed effects during dash.

All Inn

Mobile Card Game - Unity - C# - 2025
12 students - 1 month

  • This project was made during my second year at ISART Digital.

  • I worked in a team of 5 Gameplay Programmers, 5 Game Designers and 2 Sound Designers.

  • All Inn is a card game that takes place in an inn.
    Align your cards with your opponent's to have a higher value and win the fight.
    Visit the shop to get new cards with powerful effects,
    and perks that will make your run unique!

My Contributions

  • Shop Creation

    • Created the shop visited between each match.

    • Coded boosters containing cards from a random pool, as well as random perks.
  • Card and Perk Effects Dynamic Display

    • Created a popup to show card and perk effects, with icons and text
      changing dynamically.

    • Used Unity's localization tables to get texts from both English and French.

    • Integrated 3D drink models that show which perks you have earned.

Blasting Cars

2D One Button Racing Game - Godot - C# - 2024
1 student - 1½ week

  • This project is my third work as a student at ISART Digital Paris.

  • Blasting Cars is a 1v1 racing game to play against a friend!
    It's a one button game that aims to be fun and easy to play.

  • Hold to accelerate, but beware of overheating!
    You can cool down your car near a 💧water spot,
    and double tap to send a 🔥fireball to your opponent!

    • Player inputs management for different actions(Simple Press, Double Press, Hold)

    • Car movement and acceleration

  • Game Design

    • Conceptualized an easy to play game

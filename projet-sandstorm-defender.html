Sandstorm Defender

Unity - C# - PC - November 2025
6 students - 10 days

  • This game was made as part of the Ubisoft Volunteer Week Game Jam.
  • The theme was "Weathering the Storms: From Preparation to Action to Reconstruction", in partnership with the NGO Direct Relief.
  • Help Osiris, the God of life, protect your village against Seth, the God of storms, who summons waves of tornadoes.
  • Deflect the tornadoes' path by blowing on them so they don't hit your structures!

Rockoban

2D Puzzle Game - Godot - C#

This project was part of my first year as a student at ISART Digital Paris.
We worked on it for about one month and a half, as a group of 5 students.

My Contributions

  • Juiciness and Game Feel

    I worked on all the lights / projector effects, as well as squash and stretch effects for in-game objects.
    Some 2D assets are of my creation, such as the box and projector sprites, or the gold / platinum / diamond disks on the victory screen.
    I also worked on some particle effects.

  • Optimisation des performances
  • Ajout des animations et effets visuels
